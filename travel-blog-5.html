August, 2023

Omkareshwar
Jyotirling Omkareshwar - Mamleshwar - Mahakaleshwar - indore, Madhya Pradesh

Embarking on a two-day spiritual odyssey from Pune with my husband and our 20-month-old daughter, our weekend trip to the Jyotirling temples of Madhya Pradesh, including Omkareshwar, Mamleshwar, and Mahakaleshwar, promised not only divine blessings but also moments of deep introspection and family bonding. With a flight to Indore marking the beginning of our journey, we eagerly anticipated the spiritual experiences that awaited us in the heart of Madhya Pradesh.

As we landed in Indore, the bustling city welcomed us with open arms, its vibrant energy setting the tone for our adventure ahead. Quickly making our way towards Omkareshwar, we felt a sense of anticipation building with each passing mile. Upon arriving at the sacred island temple, surrounded by the gentle flow of the Narmada River, we were struck by the serene beauty of the Jyotirlinga, a beacon of divinity amidst the tranquil waters.

Continuing our pilgrimage, we crossed over to the nearby Mamleshwar Temple, where ancient stone structures and echoing chants enveloped us in a spiritual embrace. Despite the challenges of traveling with a toddler, our daughter's curious eyes and innocent laughter added a special joy to our journey. With hearts full of reverence and gratitude, we offered our prayers as a family, cherishing these moments of togetherness and shared spirituality.

The following day, our quest led us to Mahakaleshwar, the revered abode of Lord Shiva in his fierce form. As we approached the temple, a palpable sense of devotion filled the air, drawing us into the heart of the spiritual pilgrimage. Amidst the throngs of devotees, we found solace and inspiration, reaffirming our faith and commitment to the path of spirituality. With each temple visit, we not only received blessings but also gained a deeper understanding of ourselves and our connection to the divine. Additionally, our journey included a visit to the Kal Bhairav Temple, where we sought the blessings of the fierce manifestation of Lord Shiva, further enriching our spiritual experience in the sacred land of Madhya Pradesh as a family.

February, 2019

New Delhi
Delhi

Living in Noida provided the perfect base for frequent trips to Delhi, where shopping excursions to Sarojini Nagar and Chandni Chowk became regular adventures. Sarojini Nagar offered trendy, affordable fashion, while Chandni Chowk’s historic lanes were a treasure trove of textiles, jewelry, and mouth-watering street food.

The Delhi Metro was a game-changer, making it convenient to explore the city’s vast expanse. It was not just a mode of transport but a window into the daily life of Delhiites, offering a comfortable and efficient way to navigate the bustling metropolis.

Weekends often meant visiting iconic landmarks like Delhi Gate, a historic entry point to Old Delhi, and the serene Bangla Sahib Gurudwara, known for its peaceful atmosphere and community kitchen. These excursions provided a mix of historical insights and spiritual calm, enriching the urban exploration experience.

Delhi's allure was further enhanced by its diverse cultural scene and vibrant food offerings. From historical sites like India Gate and Qutub Minar to the bustling Hauz Khas Village, each visit uncovered new layers of the city's charm, making every trip memorable and unique.

17

May, 2018

Rishikesh-Haridwar
Haridwar, Uttarakhand

Embarking on an exhilarating trip with office colleagues, we ventured to the enchanting towns of Rishikesh and Haridwar, nestled along the banks of the majestic Ganges. Our itinerary promised an adrenaline-fueled adventure with camping, river rafting, and nights filled with dance around crackling bonfires, along with the spiritual experience of witnessing the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti in Haridwar.

Upon arrival, we were greeted by the soothing melody of the flowing river and the verdant beauty of the surrounding Himalayan foothills. Setting up camp amidst nature's embrace, we eagerly prepared for the thrill of river rafting, a heart-pounding experience that would test our teamwork and courage against the Ganges' swirling rapids.

As we navigated the rushing waters, our hearts raced with excitement and exhilaration, each rapid conquered filling us with a sense of triumph and camaraderie. Adrenaline coursed through our veins as we paddled through the twists and turns of the river, the stunning scenery adding to the intensity of the experience.

After our adventures in Rishikesh, we made our way to Haridwar to witness the awe-inspiring Ganga Aarti, a spiritual ceremony that left us spellbound. As the evening descended and the banks of the Ganges came alive with the glow of flickering lamps, we were immersed in a sense of divinity and tranquility, the rhythmic chants echoing in our hearts long after the ceremony had ended.

December, 2023

Nainital-Jimcorbett
Nainital-Jimcorbett, Uttarakhand

Setting off on an adventurous escapade with my husband and our close friends, we ventured from Delhi to Nainital and Jim Corbett National Park, with thrilling stops along the way. Our itinerary promised a blend of nature's serenity, wildlife encounters, and adrenaline-pumping activities, including paragliding in Bhimtal.

As we left the hustle and bustle of Delhi behind, the anticipation of our journey filled the air with excitement. The scenic drive to Nainital, nestled amidst the Kumaon hills, offered breathtaking views of lush forests and sparkling lakes. Upon arrival, we were captivated by the charm of Naini Lake and the quaint streets of Mall Road, where we indulged in local delicacies and shopped for souvenirs.

Next, our adventure took us to Jim Corbett National Park, where we embarked on thrilling safaris to spot majestic tigers and other wildlife species in their natural habitat. The vast expanse of the jungle, echoing with the calls of exotic birds and animals, filled us with a sense of wonder and awe.

In Bhimtal, we soared to new heights as we experienced the exhilaration of paragliding over the serene lake and lush landscapes below. The feeling of freedom and adrenaline rush as we glided through the sky will remain etched in our memories forever.

As our trip came to an end, we returned to Delhi with hearts full of cherished moments and unforgettable experiences. From the tranquility of Nainital to the thrill of Jim Corbett and the adventure of paragliding in Bhimtal, our journey was a perfect blend of relaxation, excitement, and bonding with loved ones.
