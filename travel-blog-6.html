Shimla-Manali-Kasol
Shimla-Manali-Kasol, Himachal Pradesh

Embarking on our first trip together after marriage, my husband and I ventured into the picturesque landscapes of Shimla, Manali, Kasol, and Manikaran. With excitement bubbling in our hearts, we set out to explore the scenic wonders of Himachal Pradesh, eager to create lasting memories in each destination. From the charming streets of Shimla to the snow-capped peaks of Manali and the serene vibes of Kasol, our journey was a delightful tapestry of adventure and romance.

In Shimla, we strolled hand in hand through the Mall Road, savoring the crisp mountain air and indulging in local delicacies. The colonial charm of the town, coupled with panoramic views of the surrounding hills, painted a picture-perfect backdrop for our newfound companionship. As we ventured further into the lush greenery of Manali, we were mesmerized by the breathtaking vistas of snow-capped mountains and cascading waterfalls, forging unforgettable memories amidst nature's splendor.

Celebrating my husband's first birthday after marriage with our dear friends at Manikaran Gurudwara was a heartwarming experience filled with love and gratitude. Amidst the tranquil surroundings of the gurudwara, we found solace and spiritual enlightenment, strengthening the bonds of friendship and camaraderie. As we reflected on the beauty of our journey together, we realized that every moment spent exploring new destinations and celebrating special occasions was a testament to the love and joy that we shared as partners in life's adventures.

Dharamshala-Mcloedganj
Dharamshala-Mcloedganj, Himachal Pradesh

Embarking on a family trip from Delhi to Dharamshala and McLeod Ganj via Pathankot, we were filled with anticipation for the adventure that awaited us amidst the serene Himalayan landscapes. With my parents and brother as companions, our journey was not just about reaching a destination but also about the shared experiences and moments of togetherness along the way. As we set off on the road, the scenic beauty of the countryside served as a picturesque backdrop to our family bonding.

Arriving in Pathankot, we continued our journey towards the tranquil hills of Himachal Pradesh. The winding roads and verdant valleys guided us towards our destination, offering glimpses of the majestic mountains looming in the distance. Each passing mile brought us closer to Dharamshala, where we were eager to immerse ourselves in the rich culture and spirituality of the region.

Upon reaching Dharamshala and McLeod Ganj, we were greeted by the serene ambiance and vibrant atmosphere that permeated the towns. From exploring the bustling markets filled with Tibetan handicrafts to visiting the ancient monasteries and temples, every moment was filled with wonder and discovery. Together, we embraced the tranquility of the Himalayas and created cherished memories that would last a lifetime, reaffirming the bonds of family and the joy of exploring new horizons together.

Jan, 2019

Puri-Bhuvneshwar
Puri-Bhuvneshwar, Orrisa

Embarking on a heartfelt journey from Delhi to Bhubaneswar, my mother and I set out for a one-day trip filled with spiritual enrichment and cherished moments. As we landed in the vibrant city of Bhubaneswar, known for its ancient temples and rich cultural heritage, anticipation filled the air. With my mother's unwavering devotion as our guiding light, we embarked on a pilgrimage to seek blessings from the divine.

Our first stop was the sacred Lingaraj Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, where the grandeur of the towering spires and intricate carvings left us in awe. Amidst the hustle and bustle of devotees, my mother's serene demeanor and fervent prayers resonated deeply, reminding me of her unwavering faith and love for the divine. Together, we immersed ourselves in the spiritual ambiance, finding solace and strength in the sanctity of the temple grounds.

Continuing our pilgrimage, we visited the renowned Konark Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, where the architectural marvels and intricate sculptures left us spellbound. The magnificence of the temple, with its intricate carvings depicting mythological tales and celestial beings, served as a testament to the artistic brilliance of ancient India. As we marveled at the beauty of Konark, my mother's reverence for the divine radiated, infusing our journey with a profound sense of spirituality.

Our final destination was the majestic Konark Sun Temple, a masterpiece of Odishan architecture and a symbol of devotion to the sun god Surya. As we wandered through the temple complex, marveling at its intricate carvings and breathtaking sculptures, my mother's love for Lord Jagannath and her unwavering devotion to the divine became palpable. Together, we offered our prayers and sought blessings for our journey ahead, grateful for the opportunity to experience the sacredness and beauty of Konark.

With hearts full of gratitude and reverence, we bid farewell to Bhuvneshwar and Konark, our spirits uplifted and our souls enriched by the blessings of our divine journey. As we returned to Delhi, the memories of our one-day trip lingered, a testament to the enduring power of faith, love, and devotion.
