North Goa
North Goa

Setting off on a thrilling girls' trip from Delhi to North Goa, our adventure began with a flight to Mumbai followed by a scenic train journey to the sunny shores of Goa. With excitement coursing through our veins, we eagerly anticipated the vibrant nightlife, exhilarating water activities, and the freedom of exploring on scooters.

Arriving in North Goa, we were greeted by the pulsating energy of the coastal town. Our evenings were filled with laughter and music as we explored the eclectic array of pubs and beach clubs, immersing ourselves in the lively atmosphere of Goa's nightlife scene.

During the days, we indulged in adrenaline-pumping water activities, from the thrill of parasailing high above the azure waters to the excitement of banana rides and the freedom of zipping around on scooters rented from local vendors. Each moment was a testament to the joy of living in the moment and embracing adventure.

Our accommodation, a cozy Airbnb apartment, provided the perfect sanctuary for rest and relaxation after action-packed days. Nestled amidst the palm trees, it offered a tranquil retreat where we could recharge before diving back into the excitement of Goa's vibrant streets.

As our unforgettable trip came to an end, we returned to Mumbai and then to Delhi, hearts full of cherished memories and spirits uplifted by the magic of Goa's beaches, nightlife, and boundless sense of adventure.

South Goa
South Goa

Beginning a memorable family trip from Pune to South Goa, my husband, our 20-month-old daughter, and I eagerly anticipated days filled with sun, sand, and exciting water activities. Our journey began with a scenic drive, winding through lush landscapes until we reached the pristine shores of South Goa.

Arriving at our destination, we were greeted by the gentle lull of the waves and the warm embrace of the tropical sun. While our daughter delighted in building sandcastles and splashing in the shallow waters, my husband and I sought adventure in the azure sea.

Underwater diving offered us a glimpse into a vibrant world beneath the surface, where colorful corals and exotic marine life danced before our eyes. The experience was nothing short of magical, leaving us in awe of the wonders of the ocean.

For an adrenaline rush, we indulged in couple parasailing, soaring high above the coastline hand in hand, with the gentle breeze kissing our faces and the panoramic views stretching out below us. The thrill of jet skiing added to the excitement, as we sped across the sparkling waters, feeling the spray of the sea on our skin.

As the sun set on each day of our unforgettable trip, we found solace in the simple joys of family time, sharing laughter and creating cherished memories together. Our journey to South Goa was not just about the adventures we experienced but also about the bond we strengthened as a family, making it a trip we would always hold dear in our hearts.
